Welcome to Albania

Tucked between the picturesque Albanian Alps and the stunning Adriatic Sea lies the small yet mighty country of Albania. If you're a lover of history, architecture, seafood, and all things Mediterranean, then Albania has everything you need for a summer (or winter) packed full of things to do. From the vibrant streets of the capital Tirana and the oft-overlooked Adriatic and Ionian coastlines, to the ancient ruins of Butrint and the Ottoman architecture of Berat‚ÄîAlbania promises to enchant.

See some of the most beautiful destinations to visit>>>

Valbona

Valbona Valley is a pristine paradise hidden within the Albanian Alps, offering rugged mountain landscapes and tranquil rivers. The valley is renowned for its breathtaking hiking trails, which wind through dense forests and open up to panoramic views of towering peaks. Valbona's untouched nature and serene beauty make it a perfect escape for adventurers and nature lovers alike.

Saranda

Saranda, nestled along the Ionian coast, boasts stunning beaches with crystal-clear waters that invite you for a refreshing dip. The city is known for its lively promenade, offering breathtaking views of the nearby Greek island of Corfu. Rich in history, Saranda is also home to ancient ruins like the Butrint National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Berat

Berat, known as the "City of a Thousand Windows," is a UNESCO World Heritage site with a rich history and stunning Ottoman-era architecture. The city's white-washed houses climb up the hillsides, creating a picturesque scene against the backdrop of Mount Tomorr. Walking through its cobblestone streets, you'll discover theiconic Berat Castle, which offers sweeping views of the surrounding valley.

Tirana

Tirana, Albania's vibrant capital, is a city that blends history with modernity. Colorful buildings, lively squares, and a dynamic arts scene make it a hub of culture and creativity. Explore the city's landmarks, such as Skanderbeg Square, the National History Museum, and the Pyramid, or enjoy the thriving caf√© culture and bustling nightlife that reflect Tirana's youthful energy.
